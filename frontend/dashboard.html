🛡️ Safe Apply Strategy

The right way to apply without risking your LinkedIn account

⛔ Why We Don't Automate LinkedIn Directly

Direct LinkedIn automation violates ToS Section 8.2 and carries serious risks:

  • ⛔ Permanent account ban
  • ⛔ IP address blacklisting
  • ⛔ Profile removed from search results
  • ⛔ Legal action under CFAA
  • ⛔ Auto-rejected by ATS filters that detect bot applications

The JSearch API we use is a licensed data aggregator — fully legal:

  • ✅ Licensed LinkedIn data partner
  • ✅ Covers LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter
  • ✅ No account risk whatsoever
  • ✅ AI-tailored applications get 3–5× more callbacks
✅ The 5-Step Safe Workflow
1

Agent Discovers & Scores Jobs

Runs daily at 9 AM (or on demand). Searches JSearch + Remotive APIs for your target roles in USA and Switzerland. Claude AI scores each job 0–100% against your resume.

2

AI Tailors Your Resume

For every job scoring above your threshold (default 65%), Claude rewrites your resume to mirror the job description's exact keywords. ATS scanners rank tailored resumes 60–80% higher.

3

Cover Letter Generated

A targeted, company-specific cover letter is generated for each job. 3 paragraphs: hook, proof, call to action. Not generic. References specific things about the company and role.

4

WhatsApp Summary Sent

You receive a daily WhatsApp message with your top matches ranked by score. Each message includes the company, role, score, and direct application link.

5

You Apply — 2 Minutes Per Job

Open the Job Queue tab. Click a job. Your tailored resume and cover letter are one-click-copy-ready. Paste into the application form. Click Apply. Mark as applied in dashboard. Done in 2 minutes — and recruiters can tell it's a human, quality application.
